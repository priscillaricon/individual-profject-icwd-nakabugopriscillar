This is a multi-page website dedicated to raising awareness about **domestic violence**, providing support resources for survivors, and outlining measures for individuals, families, and the community to get involved in prevention. The website emphasizes empowerment, safety, and creating a future free from violence.

---

## üöÄ Website Overview

The site is structured into several key pages, each focusing on a different aspect of the organization's mission and the topic of domestic violence.

Content Focus |
| background (AboutUs) | **Our Mission, Vision, Values, and Objectives.** Details the organization's core beliefs, team, and goals for promoting awareness and supporting victims. |
 | Introduction (Overview) | **Introduction to Domestic Violence** and the organization. Includes key statistics (50% of women experienced violence, 14,073 cases reported in 2024), a call to action, and an incident report form. |
| measures (Get Involved) | **How to Stop the Violence.** Provides actionable steps for individuals, families, and the community to intervene, support survivors, and challenge the culture of violence. |
| More insights (About Violence) | **Detailed information on Domestic Violence.** Defines it, lists its types and forms (Physical, Emotional, Sexual, Economic, Psychological, Digital, Verbal), discusses its impact, and outlines signs to look for. |
| Contact Us - Balance For All | **Support and Contact Information.** Lists 24/7 hotline numbers (MTN: **0764164518**, AIRTEL: **0753381526**), a text number (**08000**), and options for live chat. |

## ‚ú® Key Features & Goals

* **Awareness and Education:** Defines domestic violence and outlines various types and signs (`more.html`).
* **Data and Statistics:** Highlights the scale of the problem with impactful statistics (`overview.html`).
* **Actionable Steps:** Offers clear, categorized ways for different groups (Individual, Family, Community) to get involved in prevention and support (`measures.html`).
* **Survivor Support:** Prioritizes providing **24/7 accessible contact methods** for immediate and confidential help (`contact.html`, `measures.html`).
* **Organizational Transparency:** Clearly states the mission, vision, and core values that drive the organization (`index.html`).
